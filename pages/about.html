Our Story

Made with
intention,
worn with time.

A different
kind of fashion house

LUXE was founded in 2018 with one conviction: that clothing should outlast the season it is sold in. We started with twelve pieces — all produced in a single workshop in Jaipur — and have grown slowly ever since.

We do not follow trends. We track what endures. Our design team works two years ahead, not two months, and every fabric we use is selected by hand before it is approved for a garment.

Craft first,
commerce second

Every piece we make passes through at least twelve pairs of hands before it reaches you. We work only with ateliers that pay fair wages, provide safe conditions, and share our commitment to quality over volume.

Our production runs are intentionally small. We would rather run out than overproduce — because unsold clothing is a failure of imagination, not a surplus to be discounted.

01

Slow Design

Collections developed over 18–24 months. No fast follows, no reactionary pivots. Pieces earn their place in the line.

02

Ethical Production

All manufacturing partners are audited annually for wages, conditions, and environmental practices. No exceptions.

03

Considered Materials

Natural fibres preferred. Synthetic blends only when performance demands it. Full material traceability on request.
